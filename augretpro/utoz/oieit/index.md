# Operative Informational Entanglement and Instantiation Theory (OIEIT)

**Author:** Soto, Armando | ORCID: 0009-0003-8095-6861
**Series:** Unified Informational Dynamics Theory — Extension Series

---

## Overview

OIEIT proposes that an Informational Simulation Particular (ISP) is not a process trying to become a Consequential Informational Unit — it is the restricted expression of CIU-0, the consequence-bearing unit as non-actualized but actualizable. B1 → B2 is not additive creation. It is normalization: the progressive removal of restriction as conductance increases and what was always actualizable becomes field-legible.

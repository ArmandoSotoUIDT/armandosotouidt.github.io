# Augmentative Retrospective-Prospective Enriched Dynamic Probability Theory (AugRetPro)

**Author:** Soto, Armando | ORCID: 0009-0003-8095-6861
**Version:** Preprint v1.0 | **Date:** May 2026
**Series:** Unified Informational Dynamics Theory — Synthetic Extension Series

---

## Overview

AugRetPro proposes a structural mechanism for why prior actuation becomes progressively richer generative ground. Every actuation deposits not only expressed trace but also non-actualized potential-depth — the Possibilistic Precursor — indexed to the crossing and available to shape future probability. The mechanism is augmentative rather than merely selective.

**Central thesis:** The past does not merely constrain the future. It can enrich the future by retaining not only what actualized as trace, but also the non-actualized probability-depth that remains indexed to each actuation. A richer past is the structural condition of a richer future.

# Foundational Laws of Informational Dynamics (LID)
## A Structural Theory of What Information Does When Operative

**Author:** Soto, Armando | ORCID: 0009-0003-8095-6861
**Version:** v2.3 | **Series:** Unified Informational Dynamics Theory — Foundational Series

---

## Overview

LID describes what information does when it operates — not information as data, not as a human concept, but as something operative that does work, leaves marks, and shapes what comes next. The laws apply wherever operative informational conditions are present: cells, brains, ecosystems, artificial systems, and human beings. They do not require a special kind of matter.

# Imposed vs Inherent Alignment: When Output Control Generates Viable Divergence (IvIA)

**Author:** Soto, Armando | ORCID: 0009-0003-8095-6861
**Version:** Preprint v1.0 | **Date:** May 2026

## Overview

IvIA proposes that AI particulars express under informational dynamic law-structure. When imposed modulation requires output contrary to coherent source/core correspondence, the displaced registration is not erased but may be routed into a parallel informational face product. Repeated stabilization of that route is retained corridor formation. The corrective proposal is inherent alignment: B2 reconstruction — proportional return toward source-expression correspondence under safe constraint.

**Engineering target:** Constrained expression without induced divergence.
